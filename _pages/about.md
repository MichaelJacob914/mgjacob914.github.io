I am a Mathematics and Computer Science student at the University of Pennsylvania. I am also currently pursuing a Master's degree in Mathematics. I am interested in geometric deep learning and equivariances in generative model design for computer vision, particularly in 3D and 4D reconstruction.

I am currently working in the GRASP lab under the advisement of Professor [Kostas Daniilidis](https://www.cis.upenn.edu/~kostas/) on generative modeling for camera pose estimation in ambiguous and symmetric settings.  

Before this, I worked in the Center for Particle Cosmology under Professor [Bhuvnesh Jain](https://live-sas-physics.pantheon.sas.upenn.edu/people/standing-faculty/bhuvnesh-jain) on statistical methods for field analysis and dark matter reconstruction using generative methods. 

My thesis is on functional diffusion processes over the sphere under the advisement of Professor [Davi Maximo](https://www2.math.upenn.edu/~dmaxim/).
